A Message from the Owner About
the Business Transition at Cynergy E-Bikes


Dear Cynergy Customers,
It is with a heavy heart that after 10 years in business we are throwing in the towel on our retail e-bike business. We simply cannot compete with the internet and Amazon. Cynergy has been our baby for five years, ever since we purchased it from the original owners, but we have no choice but to move on. We wanted to personally thank you all your support and business over the years. 

In our tenure in the E-bike world, we have met so many wonderful people and we have reduced carbon emissions while doing it. So, hurray to all of you for doing your part for the planet. 

While we are ending our retail bike business, we will continue to offer services to our customers. 
We will continue to service bikes at our current location until we find a business to sublease our space. At that point we will move to a new smaller and less expensive location. We encourage you to make appointments to take care of any comp or regular tune-ups.  

Additionally, we are having a huge sale beginning of December 1st. Everything will be 20%-50% off. That includes E-bikes, helmets, apparel, locks, tires, and small pets. We have enjoyed our time in the E-bike business, and we wish you all the best! Our December hours will be reduced to Thursday through Sunday, but we will also make special arrangements for those that cannot make these reduced hours. 

Thank you and with great gratitude.
 Sami Khawaja, Owner
