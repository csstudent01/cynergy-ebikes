Your E-Bike Repair Experts

E-bikes have special needs, and we are committed to providing top-notch e-bike electrical system service and support for every e-bike we sell. Our mechanics have the experience and diagnostic tools needed to keep you riding smoothly. (Sorry, but we are unable to provide electrical service for e-bikes we do not sell.)

E-bike services that we provide to our customers for the bikes we sell include:

  • Battery, display, & controller replacements & upgrades
  • E-bike integrated lighting systems
  • Electrical system diagnostics & troubleshooting
  • E-bike system software & firmware updates


Mechanical repair services we offer for most bicycles (not just e-bikes) include:

  • Flat repair
  • Brake adjustments and pad replacement
  • Hydraulic brake service
  • Derailleur adjustment
  • Cable adjustment and replacement
  • Component installation
  • Wheel truing

Talk to one of our mechanics about how we can help keep you riding year-round.

Brands and Bikes We Service

When you bring in an e-bike for service, please bring in your battery & key. If you suspect the issue is related to your charger, please bring your charger in also.

We service all Bosch e-bike systems.

E-bike brands that we stock and provide full service support for are:

  • Bianchi
  • Dahon
  • Giant
  • Momentum
  • Bulls
  • EG
  • Liv
  • Cube
  • iZip
  • Magnum

E-bike brands we no longer carry but will provide service for existing customers are:

  • Diamondback
  • Scott
  • Haibike
  • Raleigh

If your e-bike is not listed above it is highly unlikely we will be able to provide electrical system service for you due to training and parts availability.
